Being sidelined is forcing Europe toward strategic autonomy. Crisis-driven integration historically succeeds where planned integration fails.

5. India's 50% Tariff Is A Gift To China

Pushing India toward Russia-China accelerates the exact multipolar world the US seeks to prevent. Tactical wins creating strategic losses.
